ABOUT ME

Hello, my name is Daniel! I am an undergraduate student majoring in computer science with an interest in physics at UMBC. I am passionate about deep learning research, software development, and science. I seek to build a career developing complex decision making systems that are of particular benefit to science and engineering.


My current research focuses on Deep Learning for Nuclear Fission Power Plant (NPP) Optimization which I have been conducting for the last 8 months @ Massachussetts Institute of Technology (MIT). I also have experience in computer vision, cybersecurity, and robotics. Welcome to my personal website. Here, you can find details about who I am and what I do. I have gained experience in a variety of different languages, platforms, and technologies, while also trying to do some good in the world along the way.
