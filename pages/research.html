What I'm Passionate About

Thus far, I have conducted research at Massachussetts Institute Of Technology, Johns Hopkins Applied Physics Laboratory, and University of Maryland, Baltimore County. My current research interest is in Artificial Intelligence applied to control Nuclear Fusion Tokamak Reactors. I would love to develop AI-based control systems to make nuclear fusion energy a reality to fight Climate Change. As it relates to computer science though, my interests span AI/ML, Evolutionary Computing, and cybersecurity. In the future, I plan to earn a Ph.D. in computer science furthering and contributing to scientific progress through research.
